Implementation Recommendation

Best Options:

  • Variation 1 (Line Icons) - Most professional and scalable, works well at any size
  • Variation 3 (Typography) - Easiest to implement, no external dependencies, very lightweight
  • Variation 5 (Rounded Icons) - Best balance of clarity and modern design

For the Hotlist app, I'd recommend Variation 1 or 5 as they provide clear visual distinction while maintaining a flat, modern aesthetic. The SVG icons can be inlined directly in the HTML for best performance.

Color suggestions: Keep notes neutral (gray), schedule blue, archive purple, and delete red for consistency with common UI patterns.
