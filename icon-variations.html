Implementation Notes

Each variation uses only HTML/CSS without external dependencies. The letter-based badges (Variation 2) and status dots (Variation 4) would be the easiest to implement and most performant. The geometric shapes provide good visual differentiation while maintaining a flat, modern aesthetic.

Recommended: Variation 2 (Letter Badges) for clarity and simplicity, or Variation 4 (Status Dots) for minimal visual footprint.
