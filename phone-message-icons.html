Implementation Notes

These SVG icons are scalable and can be easily customized with CSS. The stroke-width, colors, and sizes can be adjusted to match your design system. Variation 2 (Regular Line Icons with 2px stroke) provides the best balance between visibility and elegance.

Recommended: Variation 2 or 5 for optimal clarity and modern aesthetics. These work well at various sizes and maintain good contrast.
